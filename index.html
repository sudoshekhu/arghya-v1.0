About ARGHYA

ARGHYA is a community initiative dedicated to organizing Chhath Puja celebrations with proper arrangements for devotees including ghats, lighting, safety, prasad distribution and cultural programs.

Chhath Puja Schedule

Nahay Khay

Holy bath and preparation for the vrat.

Kharna

Evening fasting and prasad ceremony.

Sandhya Arghya

Offering to the setting sun at the ghat.

Usha Arghya

Morning offering and completion of the festival.
