Kharna

A day-long fast, broken in the evening with kheer (rice pudding), roti, and fruits after sunset.

Sandhya Arghya

The main day, with devotees offering prayers and offerings to the setting sun at riverbanks.

Usha Arghya & Paran

Offering to the rising sun, followed by breaking the fast (Parana) with prasad.
